Fit Check

  1. UX Design
  2. Product Design
Context

My project is about creating a food recipe app that can compete with Tasty. Tasty is one of the most popular food recipe app in the world. Without any new feature, it is infeasible for a food app that can compete directly with Tasty.

My role

As UX design and Product Designer . I led the entire project from the first stage of brainstorming to the last stage of testing. My UX professor, Ruta Potnis, is my mentor. Kindmond, Director at Smokin’ Dogs guided me through the process of choosing color theme and logo design for Fit- check.

Approach

I discussed with Ruta after every stage of the project to get her feedback. After the first version of the app released, I consulted to other 2 senior UX desingers to get a more diversifed feedback and keep improving on my design.

Problem Statement

I learnt that there have been enough of food recipe apps as well as food delivery apps in the market. Tasty is already one of the most popular food recipe apps in the world. It is infeasible to create a traditional food app that can compete directly with them. That’s why I decided to add a high-tech feature to our new app so that we can attract millennial users. My first idea was my app must be able to track the input nutrion and generate personalized recipes for users.


Design Process

I went through 4 steps in my design process.

01.

Empathize with users

I use 4 research methods: competitor analysis, heuristics analysis, 1-on-1 Interview, survey.

02.

Define problem

Many millennial users do not lead healthy lifestyle. They are obsessed with the calories they eat everyday.

03.

Ideate

Users can track their daily calories intake and sync Fitchek with other health app.

04.

Test and Final results

The test participant says the app is so convenient and beatifully designed!

Design Process

Empathize with Users

I used 4 research methods: competitor analysis, heuristics analysis, 1-on-1 Interview, survey. I identified Tasty, Yummly and All Recipe Diner Spinner as the competitors. I created competitor analysis and Heuristics analysis for three

I prepared a list of interview and survey questions to ask 10 participants about their cooking insights as well as their nutrition concern. I interviewed participants face to face, on phone and online.

Design Process

Define problem & Ideate Solution

I learnt that there have been enough of food recipe app and food delivery app in the market. To stand out, FitCheck must be able to track the input nutrion and generate personalized recipes for users. FitCheck is integrated with other health apps. After users input their health-related index, the app will process this data to produce a personalized diet for the users. The app also keeps tracking if the user is following the diet.

Learning

My design career outlook: This is the first project that I directly design all the details, from logo design, color scheme to UI elements. With the previous experience in product design, now I can understand thoroughly how detailed UI design works.

Technical challege is still there With the current app, users have to click “Add to tracking” manually to track their daily calories intake. To improve this app in the future, I would love to implement a high-tech feature to auto- matically track the nutrition without users’ attention.
