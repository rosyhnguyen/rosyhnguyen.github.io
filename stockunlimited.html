StockUnlimited

  1. Product Design
  2. UX Design
Context

My project is to restructure the product line, change the pricing plan and redesign the website for StockUnlimited

My role

I worked as a product design and UX designer under the supervision of the Vice President.

Type &
Duration

Type: Coporate Work
Duration: 6 months

Problem Statement

StockUnlimited is a subscription-based website that provides subscribers with unlimited downloads of stock digital content, such as stock graphics, stock images, audio. I can see the blooming potentials of subscription-based model, especially in the stock photos and audios market.

However, StockUnlimited has not reached its full potentials while other subscription-model competiors, were dominating the market at that time.

Design Process

Empathize with Users

We have used 4 reseach methods: Competitor Analysis, Survey, Focus Group and Persona

01.

Cometitor Analysis

StockUnlited has 2 diferent subscription plans: $9/month for images and $9/ month.
However, other competitors offers one flat price for all the site content, which makes users feel like it is StockUnlimited is more expensive.

02.

Survey

We sent a survey via email to customers who not recently download anything from their Stock- Unlimited subsriptions. The two major reasons for not using their accounts: (1) I only need a few images 34.8% (2) I have not been working on any creative projects for awhile 32.2%

03.

Focus group

Our team of 2 people sit down with the Chaiman and other staff from other brands to discuss Product and Price strategy for StockUnlimited

04.

Persona

Reach a conclusion from the investigations about the product.

Design Process

Define problem & ideate solutions

Product

We learn that 2 separate subscription (audio vs visual) is too complicated; hence, we decided to combine them into one single flat price subscription. Additionally, we introduced download pack that work as sample/ lead product for short-term, potential and hesitant customers.

Price

Customers think the current price is expensive and confusing.
We decided to merge image subscription and audio subscription into one single type of subscription: Unlimited content subscription.
Our three new pricing plan: $19 for monthly, $79 for annual (the key product) and $169 for 3 years access.

Licensing

The licensing policy was complicated. StockUnlimited had standard and extended license while Envato only has 1 broad commercial licence.
I simplified our licence policy with the help of colleagues from Legal department.

Redesign the website

The website needs to be redesigned to reflect product/price changes. New funtions like “Design-It-Yourself” Editor is added, which allows users to edit their visual on our website without the need of Adobe Photoshop or Adobe Illustrator.
