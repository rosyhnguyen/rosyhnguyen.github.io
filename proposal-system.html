Overview

Proposal system: A tool for electrical engineers (Submitters) to submit and manage their proposals. Submitters will have ability to search for latest codes and standards, submit, track and receive notifications about the status of their proposal submitted records.

My Role

Our direct team has 1 UX designer (me), 1 Product Owner, 1 Business Analysis, 3-5 developers.

Challenges

  • Understand the Exact Users Needs due to the complexity of Electrical industry and engineering code/standard proposing.
  • Ability to integrate and automate with other existing systems in the organization (e.g. PR and Ballot review sites).
  • Ability to get actual content (which can be up to 1,000+ pages per code) into the system. Depending on the timeline of this project and timeline of that conversion, There might be a dependency.

Problem Statement

In the current system, anyone can submit a proposal through a form and after submission, Project Managers will receive the list of proposals and manually work on them through excel, outlook.

Submitter are unable to track the submitted proposal. Project Managers are unable to communicate with the submitter regarding issues in the proposal. There is no transparency between the submitter and PM regarding the stage and status of the proposal. There is a high level of administration burden on the PM.
